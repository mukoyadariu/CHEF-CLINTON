About Me

I'm Chef Clinton, a culinary artist specializing in baking and cooking. My journey began in BOMA School in Nairobi and has since taken me to gourmet kitchens. I blend traditional recipes with modern presentation and take pride in using organic, locally sourced ingredients.

I offer bespoke baking services for events, baking classes, and dessert consultation for restaurants and cafes. Whether it’s a tiered wedding cake or a warm batch of sourdough, every creation is made with precision, passion, and creativity.

Baking Specialties

Custom Celebration Cakes

Layered, themed, and personalized cakes for weddings, birthdays, and special moments. Vegan and gluten-free options available.

Pastries & Tarts

Rustic Artisan Breads

Handcrafted sourdough, baguettes, ciabatta, focaccia, rye and multigrain loaves baked daily.

Corporate & Café Supply

Fresh daily baked goods for cafés, hotels, and corporate events with delivery options.
